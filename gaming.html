never allowed to touch his controllers - but soon after, I was gifted my own and began playing FPS games like Call

built something I'm proud to call my own, and it runs every game I throw at it. With PC games, I have obsessively
played Overwatch, CS-GO, Rainbow Six Seige, Modern Warfare, and now Valorant.
