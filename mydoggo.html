My Doggo

My love, my life, my best friend, my confidante! My dog's name is Sa-Seum, she's currently 4 years old, and a Labrador/Pit mix. I
rescued her from a shelter in Alabama and brought her back to her new home. After four years in atlanta, she's grown accustomed to
city living. The story behind her name is actually Korean, (Sa-Seum) or 사슴 means deer, like the animal! I gave her that name because
when she was a small ole pup, her fur was brown and she used to frolic around like Bambi. But because her name was hard to pronounce,
we named her Sauce for short and we actually fell in love with the name. Because she's got a really wholesome personality, sometimes
she's a sweet sauce and other times, her stubborn temper makes her hot sauce.

The time I rescued her, she was saved with her litter of siblings - almost 10 of them, and they all looked so different. I chose her
because when I walked into the kennel they were staying in, she was the little one that tucked herself into the corner. I was worried
that nobody would take her in, with her being so shy and timid. So, I picked her up (which I can barely do now, since she's 45 pounds)
and told her she has a home! But throughout the years, her personality really came up - and a big one being, she's terrified of a lot.
For an example: loud vehicles, vacuums, lawn mowers, fireworks, insects, cats, and her own farts. But luckily, she's very much food
driven and always eager to please! I'm always advocating for people to be more open-minded when it comes to more controversial breeds.

My home! My games!
